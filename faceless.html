Faceless

You play as a lone wanderer in a 2D hand-painted world, tasked with mastering the four primal emotions. You are not bound to one feeling; you must switch between them to survive. But be warned: Power comes at a cost. Every time you don a mask, you channel its strength but risk losing your own Sanity to its overwhelming influence.

Key Features Dynamic Attribute Switching: Change your stats (Speed, Jump, Attack) on the fly by swapping masks.
Sanity System: Powerful masks drain your Sanity. If you let an emotion consume you entirely (0 Sanity), you are lost to the madness.
Hand-Painted Stylized Art Style: A unique visual style inspired by storybooks and folklore.
Strategic Platforming & Combat: Use Joy to explore, Fear to evade, and Anger to conquer.

My role in this project was the core mechanincs which included switching the masks and the sanity system
